Boo

Injection molded pencil sharpner for kids.

In the class Design 4 we were tasked with making a pencil sharpner for kids suitable for simple injection molding.

The coruse was also an introduction in how to use 3D-printers for rapid prototyping.

Learning rendering and KeyShot has been a valuable tool for presenting design, both in terms of product design and mockups for GUI design.
