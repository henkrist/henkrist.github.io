SHAKE

During an internship at Itera in the summer of 2019, I worked in a multidisciplinary team at Santander Consumer Bank with the service "SHAKE".

Throughout the time of the internship, me and my team were tasked with further developing SHAKE, a service by Santander Consumer Bank, IF Insurance and FINN.no.
SHAKE is a service for everyone who are buying and/or selling 2nd hand cars in Norway.

The service had been undergoing a period with pilot tests of an MVP (picture above). Our task was to further develop the concept in more detail including ordering SHAKE, report injury and understand what SHAKE gives you.

A lot of our work with the service was concentrated around the user experience around the service, making the mobile user interface and real-world usability testing.

The internship proved to be an amazing opportunity to put my education in practice. Working in a multidisciplinary with another UX designer, several developers, a test manager, a market advisor and a dedicated project leader was a really good experience, and one I will look for in later projects.
