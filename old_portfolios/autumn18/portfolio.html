Spire

Autumn 2017

At the end of the introductory course of Industrial Design, we were tasked to make a coffee machine. The coffee machine was supposed to be inspired by Nordic Design, both within looks and functionality. My croup made Spire; a coffee machine inspired by the Nordic birch forests and minimalism.
See the product presentation

The Conductor

Spring 18

The Conductor was made in the course Prototyping of Interactive Media. In this course, we were tasked to a interactive experience for the Rockheim. The experience were aimed at teaching teens about music; how music is made or can be made. We made a conductor simulator, giving the user the ability to change the tempo with a wireless baton, and using buttons to decompose the track.
Read more about how we made it here.

PaperPlane

Spring 17

PaperPlane was made during my short career as a mechanical Engineer, before I migrated to Industrial Design, in the course Product Development. In this project I worked as a project leader and designer. In the course, we were asked to make someting that transports something (vague, I know). We decided to make a trolley that transports large amounts of newspapers in and out of a car. The legs fold automatically using motors then when approaching the back of a car, making it easy to slide it in to the trunk, much like a ambulance trolley.
